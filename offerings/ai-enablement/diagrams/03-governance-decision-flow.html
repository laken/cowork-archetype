Should This Connector Be Enabled?

The decision tree an executive (or their IT admin) should walk through before connecting any data source to an AI agent. Each decision point maps to a governance category from the AI Connectivity Framework.

Session use: Walk the client through this flow for each of their connectors. Most clients discover they made a Ring 2 decision (connecting a service) without making the Ring 3 decision (does the org authorize this data flow?). The flow makes that gap visible without making it feel punitive.

The deliverable: After walking the flow, document each connector's path through the tree in the security brief. The brief becomes an artifact the client can show their board, investors, or compliance team.
