What we do together

90 minutes building with your actual workflow.

We start from what you actually do every week – the tasks that eat time, the recurring decisions, the things you do well but wish took less effort. Then we figure out what to automate, what to delegate to an agent, and what to keep doing yourself.

Before the session

You fill out a short intake survey. What tools you use, how your mornings run, what you'd blink and have done if you could. It lets us skip the orientation and get straight to work.

In the session

We map your workflow, pick 1–2 high-leverage tasks, and build something. You drive. By the end you have a working setup.

The governance piece

Before you connect AI agents to your real data – email, Drive, CRM – you should know what's in scope. We cover that clearly, without the enterprise overhead.

Follow-on (optional)

Some people want one session. Others want help building out a full personal AI workflow over a few weeks, or want to think through team deployment. Either path is available.

What you leave with

Concrete output.

The session produces real artifacts you can use the same day.

  • → A working AI setup configured for your specific workflow – context files, connectors, and the habits that make them useful
  • → A clear map of what your AI tools can see and touch – so you can connect them to real data without guessing at the exposure
  • → At least one recurring task running on automation or agent delegation by the end of the session
  • → A prioritized list of what to automate next, ordered by time savings and complexity
Best fit

Who gets the most from this

This works best for founders and executives who are already curious about AI tools but haven't made them part of how they actually work. You don't need a technical background.

  • You use Claude, ChatGPT, or similar tools occasionally but haven't integrated them into your daily workflow
  • You have recurring tasks that feel like they should be automatable but you haven't had time to figure it out
  • You're about to start deploying AI tools across your team and want to understand the data handling before you do
  • You're a non-technical founder building with AI and hitting the walls that come with that – no specs, accumulating debt, unclear what's safe to ship
