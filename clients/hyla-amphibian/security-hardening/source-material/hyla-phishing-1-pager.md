# **Hyla Phishing Detection & Reporting Protocol**

## Version 1.0 | Andy Laken | 27 Feb 2025

## **When You Spot a Suspicious Email \- Take These Steps**

### **STEP 1: DON'T INTERACT**

* **Never** click links, open attachments, or reply
* **Don't** forward to colleagues or enter any information
* **Keep calm** and follow the reporting steps below

### **STEP 2: REPORT TO GOOGLE**

1. View the suspicious email in Gmail (opening the email to read it is safe)
2. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner
3. Select "**Report phishing**"
4. This sends the email to Google and moves it to your spam folder

### **STEP 3: REPORT TO OUR SECURITY TEAM**

1. GMAIL users: With the email still open, click the three-dot menu (⋮) again, then select "**Forward**"
2. Email client users (Apple Mail, Outlook, etc.) \- find the "**Forward**" option for the message
3. Send to: security@hylafunds.com
4. In the forwarded email:
* Subject line: Add "PHISHING ATTEMPT:" before the original subject
* Add a brief description explaining why it seems suspicious
* Example: "This email claims to be from Andrew but uses unusual language and asks for payment information"

**Note for Technical Users:** If you know how to view email headers in your client, please include these in your report as they contain valuable information for our security team.

### **STEP 4: SLACK NOTIFICATION (Optional)**

* Post a notification in the #risk-cybersecurity Slack channel
* Only share a screenshot if requested by security team
* Include the sender address and brief description of the threat

### **WHAT TO WATCH FOR**

Be especially alert for emails that:

* Request financial transactions or account changes
* Create urgency ("Do this immediately")
* Have unusual sender addresses (look closely at domains)
* Contain unexpected attachments or payment requests
* Show poor grammar, spelling, or unusual formatting
* Ask for passwords or personal information

### **REMEMBER**

* Even sophisticated emails can be phishing attempts
* When in doubt, report it
* It's better to report a legitimate email than miss a phishing attempt
* Our security team reviews all reports to improve our defenses

**Questions?** Contact the security team via Slack (#risk-cybersecurity) or security@hylafunds.com
