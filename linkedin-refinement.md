## Follow-ups for Next Pass

- **"On the AI side"** is an organizational transition that announces the structure. Find a way to make the shift from security to AI feel logical rather than signposted.
- **"So your firm captures the productivity gains without creating liability"** is abstract relative to the rest of the copy. Both "productivity gains" and "liability" are categories, not specifics. See if there's a more concrete way to say what the client actually gets.
- **Overlap between paragraphs 1 and 2** is reduced but not eliminated. "Help founders and their teams adopt agentic AI securely" (paragraph 1) and "help founders and their teams adopt agentic tools" (paragraph 2) are close. Monitor whether this reads as reinforcement or repetition to fresh eyes.
- **"Growth-stage" as shorthand** — works for now on LinkedIn, but revisit when the AI enablement offering is more defined. A 5-person fund managing $115M+ doesn't think of itself as growth-stage.
- **Headline and banner** — not addressed in this session. Should align with the description once it's settled.
